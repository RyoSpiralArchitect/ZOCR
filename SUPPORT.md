# Support (Draft) / サポート（ドラフト）

This document describes **support options** only. Final terms are defined in a separate agreement.
This is a **draft** and is **not legal advice**.

## Channels / 連絡先

- Email: kishkavsesvit@icloud.com
- Security reports: see `SECURITY.md` (please do not file public issues)

## Coverage / 対応時間（目安）

- Business days (JST) / 営業日（JST）で対応
- Response targets are for **initial acknowledgement + triage**, not necessarily a fix.  
  （初動=受付・切り分けの目安であり、修正/リリース時期は別途）

## Response targets (business days, JST) / 初動目標（営業日・JST）

| Severity | Definition (example) | Basic | Pro | Enterprise |
|---|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Sev1 | Production outage / data-loss risk | 1 business day | 4 business hours | 2 hours |
| Sev2 | Major functionality degraded | 2 business days | 1 business day | 8 business hours |
| Sev3 | Minor bug / questions | 5 business days | 2 business days | 1 business day |

## What’s included / 含まれるもの

- Bug triage and reproduction guidance
- Workarounds and configuration guidance (when applicable)
- Patch releases for supported versions (best-effort unless SLA is purchased)
- Security triage and guidance (see `SECURITY.md`)

## What’s not included (unless contracted) / 含まれないもの（別契約）

- On-site work / custom integration
- Bespoke domain dictionaries and data labeling
- Client-side infrastructure debugging outside Z-OCR scope

## Supported versions / サポート対象バージョン

- Best-effort for the latest released version.
- Backports can be provided by agreement (typically Pro/Enterprise).
